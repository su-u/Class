For modern society, the Internet has become an indispensable part of the infrastructure for transmitting information, and professionals who can support this future network have become indispensable, too.

To be such a professional, one needs to keep up with the continuously evolving information network technology.

And to produce even newer developments, one needs to learn not only software skills, but also about the relationship between networks and information media.

The Department of Information and Network Science trains professionals knowledgeable in computers and networks who have also learned about media, security, cognitive science, and psychology.
